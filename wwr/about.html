¡OUR HISTORY!

Over 60 Years of Experience

Two Decades of Adventure: Founded in 1995 in Argentina, Mendoza. Our company has been providing thrilling rafting experiences for adventurers and nature lovers for over two decades. Since our founding in 1995 in the heart of Argentina’s Mendoza region, we’ve been dedicated to creating exhilarating rafting experiences. With more than 20 years of expertise, we’ve honed our craft and become a trusted name in outdoor adventures.
