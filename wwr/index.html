Welcome to Mountain Mendoza Rafting!

Our Purpose

We aim to provide thrilling and safe rafting experiences for adventurers of all levels. Our expert guides ensure an unforgettable journey through the rapids, to provide exhilarating and unforgettable experiences for adventurers of all levels. Whether you're a seasoned pro or new to rafting, we have the perfect trip for you! Our motto is "Adventure awaits around every bend" From heart-pounding rapids to serene stretches of river, our expert guides will lead you through breathtaking landscapes. Picture yourself navigating the twists and turns, feeling the rush of adrenaline as you conquer each rapid. And when you’re not paddling, take in the awe-inspiring beauty of nature—the towering cliffs, lush forests, and crystal-clear waters. But it’s not just about the adventure; it’s about the shared laughter, and the stories you’ll tell around the campfire. We’re here to make it an experience you’ll treasure forever. So grab your paddle, embrace the unknown, and let’s create memories that’ll last a lifetime. Adventure awaits! let’s ride together!

What We Offer

From beginner-friendly trips to challenging white water adventures, we offer a range of rafting experiences tailored to meet your needs and excite your senses. Our expert guides are dedicated to providing you with a safe, fun-filled, and memorable experience on the river. Whether you're a first-time rafter or a seasoned adventurer, we have the perfect trip for you:

Float Trips:

Enjoy a relaxing and picturesque journey along gentle rivers, suitable for families and those seeking a tranquil experience. Take in the stunning landscapes, spot wildlife, and create lasting memories with your loved ones.

Intermediate Rapids:

For those ready to take on a bit more excitement, our intermediate trips offer the perfect balance of thrills and safety. Navigate through Class II-III rapids, learn essential rafting techniques, and work together with your team to conquer the river.
